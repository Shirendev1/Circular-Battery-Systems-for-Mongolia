# Circular Battery Systems for Mongolia

## Vision
To design a sustainable and human-centered circular battery lifecycle architecture tailored to Mongolia’s emerging industrial and environmental landscape.

## Why Mongolia?

Mongolia stands at a strategic development point.  
With increasing electric mobility trends, electronic waste growth, and mineral resource potential, the country has an opportunity to leapfrog traditional linear models and implement circular battery systems from the outset.

## Objectives

- Map the battery lifecycle within Mongolia
- Identify infrastructure and regulatory gaps
- Align with EU Battery Regulation and ESG standards
- Propose Extended Producer Responsibility (EPR) mechanisms
- Integrate AI-driven quality monitoring and traceability
- Develop a 2026–2035 roadmap

## Key Pillars

1. Circular Design & Lifecycle Mapping  
2. Policy & Regulatory Framework  
3. AI & Digital Tracking Systems  
4. Second-Life Applications  
5. Climate & Sustainability Impact  

---

This project aims to contribute to sustainable industrial development and ethical technology governance in Mongolia.
