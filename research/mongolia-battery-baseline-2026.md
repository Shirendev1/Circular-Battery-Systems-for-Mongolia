# Mongolia Battery Baseline – 2026

## 1. Current Situation Overview

Mongolia does not currently have a domestic battery manufacturing ecosystem.
Battery supply is primarily import-dependent, including:

- Automotive batteries
- Consumer electronics batteries
- Industrial batteries
- Renewable energy storage systems

## 2. Structural Gaps

- No formal national battery collection system
- Limited recycling infrastructure
- Absence of traceability mechanisms
- Environmental and soil contamination risks

## 3. Emerging Drivers

- Growing EV adoption potential
- Renewable energy expansion
- Increasing electronic consumption
- Critical mineral potential (lithium and rare metals)

## 4. Strategic Leapfrog Opportunity

Mongolia has the opportunity to design a circular battery system from the outset,
avoiding legacy linear waste models and integrating:

- Extended Producer Responsibility (EPR)
- EU-aligned regulatory frameworks
- AI-based lifecycle tracking
- Sustainable industrial planning

This document establishes the baseline context for circular battery system development in Mongolia.
